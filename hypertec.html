HYPERTEC is a voxel style dogfighter, where you control a spaceship with components! You can attach unique weapons and engines that you collect from enemies, improving your ship as you go.
The weapons are procedurally generated from a selection of different pieces, allowing for over 240 different weapons! You will be completing quests for you chosen faction, and eventually claiming the sector as your own.

Rhythm Fun was a collaberation with Caroline Stephenson, it's a rhythm game designed for young children to practice their hand eye coordination in a fun game-like context. This was our first client project as a team and we think the result was really good!

Contrelement was made for the GETA Game Jam 9. The theme was "contrasting elements" and we took it pretty literally! The game consists of sending minions of the correct opposing colour down channels to defeat the incoming enemies and destroy their spawner.

Until the World Goes Cold was a submission for the ACGS Start of Summer (SOS) and it was a bit of a longer time frame. It's a survival game where the goal is to stay alive long enough for the lake to freeze over which lets you escape. The time allowed for a more expanded game loop, with a variety of different actions and a crafting system.
